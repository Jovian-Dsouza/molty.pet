### Interactive Environment

Connected to Alexa, your pet's mood controls your room — lights, temperature, voice alerts. The environment simulates what your pet feels.

## How Monagotchi Works

"Feed my pet"→OpenClaw checks status→Recorded onchain→ESP32 /feed→Pet reacts on LCD→Alexa: green, 72°F

🤚

### Pet Your Monagotchi

5 physical buttons — feed, play, clean, sleep, heal. Press them to care for your pet. It reacts on the LCD screen in real time.

💰

### Earn or Get Slashed

Pet regularly → earn $MONA rewards on Monad. Neglect your pet → get slashed. Your care has real financial stakes.

🐾

### Pet Mood Changes

Hunger, happiness, health, energy, cleanliness — all decay over time. AI determines your pet's mood based on how you treat it.

🏠

### Room Reacts

Connected to Alexa. Happy pet = green lights, 72°F. Sick pet = red lights, 80°F. Dead pet = 85°F until reset.
